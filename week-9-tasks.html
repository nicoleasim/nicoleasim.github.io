    Task 1: Analyse Video

    letzrent

    • What is the video used for?
      • - The video is used as a moving background image for the header of the website's front page.
    • Is it used with intent?
      • - I think the video is suitable for this topic, as the video is simply showing a clip of a home. The website is a letting agency that handles all aspects of managing a rental property, and therefore the small video clip suggests the type of listing they would create for your home. Therefore, the video is used with the intent to appeal to those who are looking for that type of service and encourage them to scroll down the page to learn more.
    • What are the benefits and caveats?
      • - The only caveat is that not everyone might like it. It may make it difficult for some people with a disability to read, as the video is directly behind the text, however, they have tried to ensure the text stands out against this.
      - Overall, I think this video is beneficial for its target audience, who I assume are people looking to let their homes and likely have a knowledge of the type of service they want.
    • Is the length suitable?
      • - The length of the video is very short, and it is unlikely that a user would even finish it in its entirety before scrolling down the page. This might be beneficial as it may give the illusion of a video being longer or more interesting than it is.
      - The video is very smooth and slow. This does not disrupt the rest of the page or the information that is presented on top of it.
    • How is the video combined with other forms of media?
      • - The website makes use of text, images, and animation on their website. As the user scrolls, more text and images roll out. This allows for a seamless scrolling experience.
