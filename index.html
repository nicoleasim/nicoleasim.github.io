    Why humanities?

    Engagement

    Using interactive digital resources has been beneficial in improving student engagement. Research on the use of VR has found significant improvements in understanding and remembering of material over traditional methods (Allcoat & Munhlenen, 2018).

    Cognitive Benefits

    Research has found that children who play video games have a higher level of accuracy on cognitive tasks than children who do not (Chaarani, Ortigara, Yuan, et al. 2022). Implementing these into education could improve in areas of attention and memory.
